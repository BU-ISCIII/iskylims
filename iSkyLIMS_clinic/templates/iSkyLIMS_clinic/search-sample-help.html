Searching for a Sample

When searching for a Sample you can fill as many fields you want. When typing in a field the result are restricted to these one than match the condition.

Any typed condition makes an "AND" with the previous one

To look for a specific Sample you should enter as much as possible details you have, in order to find an exact match
In case that more than one result are matched then, a list of then are displayed.

Search Sample will display only information of the sample. The Patient related information is used with the propose to reduce the number of results

  • On Sample level
    • Looking for an especific Sample name: Filling the complete or partial sample name
    • Looking for an especific Patient: Filling the Patient name or the History Number
    • Looking for Doctor : Selecting from the samples ordered by a doctor
    • Searching for Requested Service by: Selecting from the unit which requested the service
    • Searching from an especific date: Filling the Date Field
